What we do

Mission

- To provide high school students with the skills and knowledge they need through their study, our even daily life by making discussions, events, workshops, parties, competitions in different scientific and philosophic fields
- Reaching students all over Egypt through the social media by sharing scientific, motivational videos and online courses in many fields “soft skills, sciences... etc.” To help them in easy, effective and accessible way.

vision

Our vision is to serve ungraduated students and improve their abilities and skills to build good leaders and thinkers in the future.
Being the most effective student’s activities in all over Egypt in 10 years by adding STEM Legacy touch on every student in Egypt not only in STEM schools.

goal

Students will be provided with the most needed skills for them to communicate and add their touch, by providing these skills which include English, presentation, Communication skills, and students will be provided with selfconfident which allows them to create, and succeed.

history

STEM Legacy was established on December 2015, at Dakahlia STEM School Previous activities on Dakahlia STEM School :

  • STEM Legacy party for Dakhlia STEM Students in February, 2015.
  • Scientific lecture in November, 2015.
  • Open scientific discussion in November, 2015.
  • Fun science party in February, 2015.
  • The Way To Be Event in Maady 18 Aug 2016
  • H.R Workshop in STEM Dakahlia October, 2016.
  • Marketing Training in STEM Dakahlia October, 2016.
  • Conversation Day program in STEM Schools
